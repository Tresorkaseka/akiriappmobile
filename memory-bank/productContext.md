# Product Context - akiriapp

## Why it exists
Akiri is a community-driven training platform designed for the African context. It aims to democratize access to education by allowing anyone to teach and learn, similar to Udemy or OpenClassrooms but tailored to local needs.

## User Experience Goals
- **Accessible & Inclusive**: Optimized for mobile-first users in Africa (potential low bandwidth, various devices).
- **Community Focused**: Emphasizes social learning and peer support.
- **Visual Identity**: Modern, vibrant, and premium feel using a Deep Blue (#3211d4) theme with rounded aesthetics (Stitch Design Source: `projects/9917461656842175272`).
